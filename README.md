# crowdshipai-web

**CrowdShip** — a live-streaming platform for watching (and funding) people build
software in real time. Builders vibe-code on camera; backers spend coins to shape
what gets built; recruiters watch the talent think. We take a cut of the middle.

> The atomic primitive is a video stream with someone building in front of it.

**The goal is shipping the product** — a real, working CrowdShip in front of real
people, as fast as possible. Don't reinvent solved problems; use the best existing
solution and keep moving. The one place we're never sloppy is the integrity of
value: no coin is ever created, destroyed, or moved silently.

## Where things are

- `apps/web` — the **walking-skeleton watch experience** (the visible product),
  driven by an in-memory fake behind one catalog seam.
- `packages/ledger*` — early ledger code.
